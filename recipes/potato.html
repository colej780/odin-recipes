Mashed Potatoes

Prep Time: 15 min
Total Time: 45 min

Description

Another holiday classic. This dish is just some simple, fluffly, creamy mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 medium sized russet potatoes

  • 1/2 cup of milk

  • 4 Tablespoons of butter

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel, wash, and cut potatoes into even, inch-sized cubes and place in a bowl of water to soak for 5 min

  2. Place potatoes in a pot and cover with water. Let boil until potatoes are soft

  3. Drain potatoes and mash until there is an even consistancy

  4. Add your milk and butter, stirring to incorporate until potatoes are nice and smooth

  5. Enjoy!
