Mashed Potatoes

Prep Time: 10 min
Total Time: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 5-6 medium sized russet potatoes

  • 1/2 cup of milk

  • 4 Tablespoons of butter

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes, cut into inch-sized cubes and let them soak in a bowl of water
