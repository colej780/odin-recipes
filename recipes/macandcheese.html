Mac and Cheese

Prep Time: 15 min
Total Time: 40 min

Description

This mac and cheese is super simple, easy to make, and who doesn't love mac and cheese?
Another dish my partner grew up with during the holidays, this was a no brainer when deciding
what to make for ours.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk

  • 1/2 pound Velveta

  • 2 cups shell pasta

  • 1/2 cup mexican blend cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F

  2. Cook pasta al dente according to package directions. Drain

  3. In a saucepan heat up milk until boiling and immediately reduce heat. Slowly add cubed

    Velveta while stirring until melted and creamy

  4. Stir in the pasta and spread into casserole dish. Top with mexican blend shredded cheese

  5. Bake 20 min

  6. Enjoy!
